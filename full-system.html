🔄 Placement Flow
From posting a need to filling a bed — minutes, not days.
1
Commissioner Posts Anonymous Request
60-second mobile tool or WhatsApp message. Patient needs, budget, location, urgency — all dropdowns.
60 seconds · Mobile-first
2
Matching Engine Filters Homes
Auto-filters by radius, care type, CQC rating. Only matching homes get notified.
Automatic · CQC-verified
3
Care Homes Receive Push Alert
"New placement: dementia, £850/wk, 5mi, needed Friday." Immediate notification.
Instant push notification
4
Care Homes Compete with Bids
Homes respond with price and availability. Multiple bids = competitive pricing.
30-second response
5
Commissioner Compares & Selects
Anonymous bids side by side: price, CQC, distance, availability. One-tap selection.
One-tap selection
6
Identity Revealed — Connection Made
Only NOW do both sides see each other. Direct connection to finalise. Success fee after 30 days.
Success fee on confirmed placement
🔒 Why Anonymisation Is the Model
Prevents bypass
Neither side knows who they're talking to until match is confirmed
Fair competition
Homes compete on merit — price, quality, availability — not relationships
Justifies fee
Platform creates the matching moment — same model as Upwork, Bark.com, Priceline
