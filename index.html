How It Works

Three steps. Under two hours.

SwiftPlace replaces hours of phone calls with a simple, auditable workflow.

1
📋

Create a case

Enter the placement requirements — care type, location, needs. Search the CQC directory of 15,549 registered homes and select the ones you want to reach.

60 seconds
2
🎙️

Record a voice note

Record one WhatsApp voice note with the placement details. SwiftPlace sends it to all your selected homes simultaneously. One recording, many enquiries.

30 seconds
3
✅

Homes reply, you pick

Care homes reply directly on WhatsApp — no app to download, nothing to learn. You compare responses in your dashboard and confirm the placement.

Responses in minutes
