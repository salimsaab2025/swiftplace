🔄 Placement Flow — Step by Step

The entire placement cycle — from posting a need to filling a bed — takes minutes, not days.

1

Commissioner Posts Anonymous Request

Discharge coordinator opens the app (or sends a WhatsApp message). In 60 seconds, they post: patient needs, budget, location, urgency — all via dropdowns, no typing. The request is anonymised automatically.

60 seconds · Mobile-first
2

Matching Engine Identifies Care Homes

Platform filters by radius, care type, CQC rating, and availability. Only homes that genuinely match get notified — no spam, no irrelevant alerts.

Automatic · CQC-verified
3

Care Homes Receive Push Alert

"New placement request matches your dementia unit — £850/wk budget, 5mi away, needed by Friday." Home managers see the opportunity immediately.

Instant push notification
4

Care Homes Compete with Bids

Homes respond with price and availability. "Available Thursday, £820/week, specialist dementia unit." Multiple homes bid — creating competitive pricing and fast responses.

30-second response · Competitive
5

Commissioner Compares & Selects

Anonymous responses displayed side by side: price, CQC rating, distance, availability. Commissioner taps to select the best match.

One-tap selection
6

Identity Revealed — Connection Made

Only NOW do both sides see each other's details. Direct connection to finalise the placement. Platform tracks the outcome and triggers the success fee after 30 days.

Success fee on confirmed placement

🔒 Why Anonymisation Is the Model

Problem How Anonymisation Solves It
Commissioners bypass platform after first use Can't — they don't know which home responded until they select
Care homes contact commissioners directly Can't — commissioner identity hidden until match confirmed
"Who you know" bias in placements Homes compete on merit: price, quality, availability
Justifying the success fee Platform creates genuine value — the matching moment only exists here
Proven model: Upwork, Bark.com, and Priceline all use anonymised matching to prevent bypass and justify platform fees. SwiftPlace applies the same proven mechanism to care placements.

📅 Implementation Roadmap

Four phases from first line of code to national scale. Each phase has a clear milestone before we move on.

Months 1–2
Phase 1

🛠️ MVP Build + Single Hospital Pilot

  • Build 60-second posting tool (mobile PWA)
  • Build care home response & notification flow
  • Integrate CQC data feed (auto-populated profiles)
  • WhatsApp Business channel for posting & responses
  • Anonymisation engine — identity hidden until match
  • Partner with 1 large hospital trust (free pilot)
  • Pre-register 200 care homes in 25-mile radius
  • Platform manager embedded with discharge team 2 days/wk
✅ Milestone: 50 successful placements
Months 2–3
Phase 2

📊 Proof of Concept + Evidence Pack

  • Measure: placement time, cost savings, user satisfaction
  • Build 5 detailed placement case studies
  • ROI calculation — quantified savings for hospital and homes
  • User testimonials (video) from discharge coordinators + managers
  • Iterate product based on real-world feedback
  • Begin competitive pricing data collection
✅ Milestone: Documented ROI + 80% satisfaction
Months 4–6
Phase 3

🚀 Geographic Expansion

  • Add 2 neighbouring local authorities + 1 additional hospital
  • Extend to community placements (not just discharge)
  • Care home network expands via word-of-mouth + group outreach
  • Activate success fee model (£200 floor / 2.5% of annual value)
  • Premium features beta: priority notifications, response analytics
  • Trade association partnerships (Care England, UKHCA)
✅ Milestone: 300+ active homes, revenue live
Months 7–12
Phase 4

📈 Scale & Data Moat

  • 15+ local authorities, 8+ NHS trusts
  • AI-powered matching (learning from placement success patterns)
  • Market intelligence products: pricing benchmarks, demand forecasting
  • API integrations with hospital/LA discharge systems
  • Cross-boundary placement matching
  • Data moat becomes unassailable — transaction history competitors can't replicate
✅ Milestone: £1M+ ARR, network effects accelerating
